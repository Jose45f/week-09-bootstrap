Bloody Mary

# Item Amount Average Price
1 Vodka 2oz (60ml) $25/ bottle
2 Celery Salt Large pinch $2/ Canister
3 Black Pepper Large pinch $4/ Canister
4 Fresh lemon juice 2/3oz (15ml) $1/ for 2 lemons
5 Tomato juice 3oz (90ml) $6/ Carton
6 Tabasco 6 dashes(to taste) $4/ bottle
7 Worcester sauce 2/3 oz (20ml) $8/ bottle
8 Celery Garnish Optional
9 Sherry 5-10ml $8/ 375ml bottle
Step 1

Add all of the ingredients EXCEPT the sherry to your shaker tin and fill with ice.

Step 2

Use your julep strainer to hold your ice back in the tin, and pour the cocktail in to your other, empty shaker tin. Do this back and forth about 5 times until it is diluted and aerated. OR seal your tins as usual but gently "roll" your tins around about 10 times instead of shaking.

Step 3: Enjoy!

Strain over fresh ice and float the sherry on top by pouring it in slowly. Garnish with herbs or celery (or whatever you like really!) and enjoy! (Scroll Down to follow allong!)
