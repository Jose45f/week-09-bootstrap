Moscow Mule

# Item Amount Average Price
1 Vodka 1 & 1/2 oz (45ml) $25/ bottle
2 Fresh lime Juice 3/4oz (20ml) $1 for 2 limes
3 Ginger beer 4oz (120ml) $6/ per 6
4 Lime Wedges 1 -3 pieces Optional
5 Mint Garnish Optional/td>
Step 1

Pour vodka and lime juice into a mug. (A copper mug is not neccessary but it does a good job of keeping your drink cool and its tradition! But feel free to use whatever you please.)

Step 2

Add ice cubes and ginger beer. Stir to combine.

Step 3: Enjoy!

For the garnish: lime wheel or wedge, fresh mint; if desired. (Scroll Down to follow allong!)
