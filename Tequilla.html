Tequilla Sunrise

# Item Amount Average Price
1 Tequila 2oz (60ml) $40/ bottle
2 Orange Juice 4oz (120ml) $6/ Carton
3 Grenadine 25oz (7.5ml) $4/ 12oz bottle
Step 1

Add Tequila and orange juice to shaker. Add ice then shake for 8-10 Seconds Add grenadine to your Collins Glass.

Step 2

Add Ice to glass, and strain your drink into the glass. us the back of your bar spoon to stir the bottom of the glass giving you a sunrise gradient effect on your cocktail!

Step 3: Enjoy!

Garnish with an orange wedge or wheel. Cheers!! (Scroll Down to follow allong!)
